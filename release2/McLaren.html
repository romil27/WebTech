About the Team Principal

Andreas Seidl has been serving as the team principal of McLaren since May 2019. He directly reports to the company CEO Zak Brown. McLaren hired from his previous position as team principal of Porche LMP1 Team. However, he has served in Formula 1 prior to that as head of track operations for BMW from 2000-2006, and then at BMW sauber until 2009. Since his appointment McLaren has seen significant improvement in performance. It is widely believed that he will help the team reach their earlier successes and potentially win championships in the near future.
