About the Team Principal

Toto Wolff, is the team principal of Mercedes F1 Team. Being a former racer himself, he found his calling not on the track, but in the pitlane, managing the entire operation that allows drivers to race. Currently, he holds 33% of the Merecedes F1 team, along with controlling shares in the Williams F1 team. Buying his share in Williams is how he made his entry into Formula 1. He joined the board of directors for Williams, and sold his shares in 2016. Under his leadership Merecedes has dominated the current era of F1, ever since the turbo-hybrid engines were implemented. They have won all constructors championships from 2014-2020, and only Mercedes Drivers have won the Drivers' World Championships in this era.

Toto Wolff's leadership and contribution to the team are undisputed and unmatched when it comes to guiding the team to successs and winning championships.
